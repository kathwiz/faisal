HEALTH INSURANCE

With our complete range of corporate health insurance protection, you can choose from local, regional or international health insurance schemes. Our flexible approach means we can meet the specific needs of your corporation or group, offering you a range of unique solutions from local health insurance to worldwide medical coverage.

SECURITY

Coupled to Faisal Alliance’s equity are leading first class Reinsurers that enter into risk carrying partnership with Faisal Alliance and provide pro-active financial support and expertise to the health schemes designed and written by Faisal Alliance.

SERVICE ORIENTED

Faisal Alliance’s appointment of NEXtCARE, the leading TPA & PPO in the UAE, brings with it the talents of years of hands-on experience in Health Insurance Claims Management, to ensure that group members receive the best service possible. NEXtCARE provides 24/7 contact center services for all kinds of medical insurance related queries.

OPTIMAL COST

Faisal Alliance / NEXtCARE aims to ensure that each participating member receives high-quality medical care in the most efficient and professional manner. We also assist the process of identifying and eliminating unnecessary medical services. This multi-focused approach works to support high-quality healthcare by keeping medical claims costs down, which helps to minimize work absenteeism and the burning cost per person per year.

NETWORK DIRECT BILLING

Faisal Alliance / NEXtCARE secures direct billing for in-patient and out-patient services through its contracts with all medical providers, facilities and health care professionals on both National and International level. Augmented with a 24/7 Claims Center, we facilitate our card holders with the prompt claim management services.

With product sum assured ranging from AED 50,000 to AED 2,500,000 and coverage spanning the globe, Faisal Alliance & NEXtCARE offers an extensive choice of benefits such as in-patient hospital & maternity coverage, emergency care, ambulance & evacuation services, same day surgery & diagnostic procedures. The benefits also include selection of coverage for outpatient services such as doctor’s office visits, pharmaceuticals, x-ray, scanning, laboratory services, physiotherapy as well as specialty products such as but not restricted to home care, chiropractic and osteopathy care.
